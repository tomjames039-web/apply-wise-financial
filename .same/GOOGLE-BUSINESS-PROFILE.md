# Google Business Profile Setup Guide

## Apply Wise Financial - Local SEO Setup

This guide walks you through setting up and optimizing your Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) for maximum local visibility.

---

## 1. Create/Claim Your Listing

### Step 1: Go to Google Business Profile
1. Visit [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
2. Sign in with a Google account (preferably a business email like info@apply-wise.co.uk)

### Step 2: Search for Your Business
1. Search for "Apply Wise Financial"
2. If it exists, click **Claim this business**
3. If not, click **Add your business to Google**

### Step 3: Enter Business Information
```
Business Name: Apply Wise Financial
Primary Category: Mortgage Broker
Secondary Categories:
  - Financial Planner
  - Financial Consultant
  - Loan Agency
```

### Step 4: Add Your Address
```
Address: 4 Fiddlers Hamlet, Epping, Essex, CM16 7PY
Service Area: Essex, Hertfordshire, Greater London, UK
```

**Choose "Service Area" if:**
- You visit clients at their location
- You serve clients remotely

**Choose "Physical Location" if:**
- Clients visit your office

*Recommended: Select both - show address AND define service area*

## 2. Verify Your Business

### Verification Options
Google will verify you own the business. Options include:

| Method | Time | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| Postcard | 5-14 days | Most common - postcard sent to business address |
| Phone | Instant | If available, automated call with code |
| Email | 1-2 days | If Google has verified email |
| Video | 1-3 days | Record video of location and documents |

### Postcard Verification
1. Confirm your address
2. Wait for postcard (usually 5-7 days)
3. Enter the 5-digit code from the postcard

**Important:** Don't change your business name or address until verified!

## 6. Get Reviews

Reviews are crucial for local SEO. Strategy:

### Review Request Process
1. **After completion** - Send thank you email with review link
2. **Follow up** - If no review after 7 days, gentle reminder
3. **Make it easy** - Direct link to review page

### Review Link
Your review link will be:
```
https://g.page/r/[YOUR-PLACE-ID]/review
```

Find it in your GBP dashboard under "Get more reviews"

### Email Template
```
Subject: A quick favour? 🏠

Hi [Name],

Thank you for choosing Apply Wise for your mortgage. We hope the process went smoothly!

If you have a moment, we'd really appreciate a Google review. It helps other people find us and know what to expect.

[DIRECT REVIEW LINK]

Just a few words about your experience would be wonderful.

Thank you so much!
The Apply Wise Team
```

### Responding to Reviews

**Positive Review Response:**
```
Thank you so much for the wonderful review, [Name]! It was a pleasure helping you with your mortgage. We wish you all the best in your new home. If you ever need advice in the future, we're always here to help. - The Apply Wise Team
```

**Negative Review Response:**
```
Thank you for your feedback, [Name]. We're sorry to hear about your experience and take all feedback seriously. Please contact us at info@apply-wise.co.uk so we can understand what happened and try to resolve any issues. - Apply Wise
```

## 7. Post Regular Updates

Google Posts keep your profile active. Post weekly:

### Post Types
- **What's New** - Company updates, new services
- **Offers** - Free consultations, rate updates
- **Events** - Webinars, local events
- **Products** - Highlight specific services

### Post Ideas
```
Week 1: "Interest rates update - is now a good time to remortgage?"
Week 2: "First-time buyer? Here's what you need to know about deposits"
Week 3: "Self-employed? We specialise in director mortgages"
Week 4: "Free protection review - are you paying too much?"
```

### Post Format
- Image: 1200 x 900px
- Text: 100-300 words
- CTA button: "Learn more", "Call now", "Get quote"
- Link: Relevant page on your website

## 9. Local SEO Tips

### Citations
Ensure your business is listed consistently across:
- Yell.com
- Thomson Local
- FreeIndex
- Yelp
- Facebook
- LinkedIn

**Important:** Name, Address, Phone (NAP) must be IDENTICAL everywhere.

### Local Keywords
Target these in your GBP description and posts:
- Mortgage broker Essex
- Mortgage advisor Hertfordshire
- Mortgage broker Epping
- Essex mortgage advice
- Hertfordshire mortgage broker

### Website Connection
- Link from your website to your GBP
- Embed Google Map on contact page
- Include local schema markup
- Ensure NAP matches on website
